Description^

Sunway Manor, property that features contemporary ambience that is blended with traditional South Indian hospitality. Guests can arrive at the property by Chennai International Airport which is nearest airport to the property. While Puducherry railway station is 3.8 km and Pondicherry Bus Stand is 2.8 km away. Guests can visit various prominent attractions such as Church of the Sacred Heart of Jesus(3.8 km), Eglise de Notre Dame des Anges(4.2 km), French War Memorial(4.4 km), Arulmigu Manakula Vinayagar Temple(4.7 m), Paradise Beach(6.1 km) and many other notable spots. The hotel is known for providing luxury accommodation and facilities, which are a good blend of contemporary design and comfort. For a comfortable and relaxed stay an array of facilities are offered such as Wi-Fi internet access, laundry, front desk and parking. The supportive staff of the hotel ensures that all needs of guests are timely fulfilled. The hotel presents aesthetically designed spacious rooms with a variety of accommodation options to suit leisure and business requirements. Rooms are fitted with amenities like air condition, telephone, television, hot/cold water, attached bathroom and various other essential bathroom toiletries. The In-house multi-cuisine restaurant dishes out the best of Indian and international delicacies to cajole the taste-buds. Furthermore guests can also relish evening drinks and spend some quality time with friends at the in-house bar, which is stocked with choicest spirits. The hotel also features well-equipped conferencing and banqueting venues, making it a perfect base for business travellers. Experience a grand stay and a warm hospitality at Sunway Manor!
