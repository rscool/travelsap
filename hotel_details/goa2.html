Ginger Hotel Delhi Rail Yatri Niwas IRCTC

New Delhi Railway Station, Bhav Bhutti Marg, View on map

Description^

Situated in the National Capital and Political hub of India, New Delhi. Hotel’s proximity to the Nizamuddin Railway Station (8.8 km), Patel Chowk Bus Stop (3.2 km) makes Ginger Rail Yatri Niwas – IRCTC hotel an ideal choice for transit and short stays. Indira Gandhi International Airport is the nearest airport to reach the property. The hotel features gymnasium for fast, convenient and affordable workout, that can be enjoyed by business and leisure travellers during their stay here. The hotel also offers other modern facilities like front desk, lobby, parking, room service, security, housekeeping, Wi-Fi, doctor on call and many more. Guests can choose their stay in one of the elegantly furnished rooms that offer amenities like air condition, comfy beds, clean linen, wardrobe, working desk, television, tea/coffee maker and attached bathroom with hot/cold water and essential toiletries. The multi cuisine restaurant of hotel has a daily changing menu that offers a variety of breakfast, lunch and dinner options. Guests can also use the in-room dining service on demand to have food in the privacy of their room. The hotel’s strategic location and signature hospitality, makes it an ideal stay option for business travelers. Guests can use conference hall, fitted with latest facilities. Holidaymakers can visit places like Laal Kila (5.1 km), Jama Masjid (2.2 km), Humayun’s Tomb (7.7 km) and many more. Shoppers can go to Chandni Chowk at the distance of only 2 km, it offers great variety of shops selling semi-precious jewellery, dry fruits, ethnic clothes, footwears, embroidered bags, hand-painted wall hangings etc for all. Have a comfortable stay at Ginger Rail Yatri Niwas – IRCTC Hotel!
